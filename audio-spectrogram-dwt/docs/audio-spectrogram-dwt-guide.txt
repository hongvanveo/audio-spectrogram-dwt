LAB 5 - GIAU ANH VAO AUDIO BANG SPECTROGRAM, INVERSE STFT VA DWT

Muc tieu
-------
Sinh vien thuc hien tung cong doan rieng:

  1. Tao audio goc cover.wav.
  2. Tao hoac chuan bi anh bi mat secret.png.
  3. Xu ly anh: doc anh, hoan vi bang key, chuan hoa thanh ma tran spectrogram.
  4. Xu ly tin hieu: dung Inverse STFT de bien ma tran anh thanh tin hieu mien thoi gian.
  5. Nhung tin: dung DWT tren audio goc, ghi tin hieu bi mat vao he so tan so cao, IDWT thanh stego.wav.
  6. Nghe/xem va kiem tra audio da thay doi.

Quy trinh ky thuat
------------------

  secret.png
  -> process_image.py: hoan vi bang key, tao processed_image.json
  -> istft.py: xem processed_image.json nhu spectrogram, tao hidden_signal.json
  -> dwt_embed.py: DWT cover.wav, nhung hidden_signal vao detail coefficients
  -> IDWT
  -> stego.wav

Sau moi task, co the chay checkwork de xem muc tuong ung chuyen sang Y.

Task 1 - Tao audio cover
------------------------
  cd ~/stego
  python3 generate_cover.py --out cover.wav
  checkwork

Muc can Y:
  cover_created

Task 2 - Tao anh bi mat va mo xem
---------------------------------
  python3 generate_secret_image.py --out secret.png
  ./view_secret.sh
  checkwork

Muc can Y:
  secret_image_created
  secret_image_viewed

Neu giao vien cung cap san anh, co the copy anh do vao ~/stego/secret.png thay cho lenh generate_secret_image.py.
